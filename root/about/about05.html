Lucky Lottery, Happy Society

Welcome to the website of the Korea Lottery Commission

The Korea Lottery Commission (KLC) has the authority to issue lotteries and is also responsible for formulating lottery policies and managing lottery funs to ensure that lottery proceeds can be used efficiently.

Four-leaf clovers symbolize good luck and fortune. To find a four-leaf clover, we often pass by three-leaf clovers. Ironically, however, their flower language is 'happiness.' I think that lotteries are like these three-leaf clovers.

The lottery brings good fortune to prize winners, but lottery sales revenue goes to good causes, including social welfare programs for the underprivileged. The lottery fund provides housing support to the homeless, arts and cultural opportunities to the underserved group, and high-tech wheelchairs to the disabled.
Lottery tickets purchased today deliver hope, help, and care tomorrow to the every corner of our society.

The KLC is working hard to make the lottery contribute to society by helping others, thereby creating a culture of sharing. Under the slogan of "Lucky Lottery, Happy Society", we will do our best to share happiness with as many people as possible.

Thank you.
