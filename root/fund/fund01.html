Funding Operations

  • HOME
  • >Lottery Fund
  • > Funding Operations
Lottery sales revenue - after prize payouts, operating costs and administrative expenses for issuing and managing lottery products - is transferred to the "Lottery Fund", the amount of which totals around 1.5 trillion
(US$1415 million) per year. The lottery fund is used for social welfare programs with rational and transparent standards.

Thirty-five percent of lottery proceeds are appropriated to 10 legally mandated projects in accordance with Article
56 of the Lottery and Lottery Fund Act; the remaining 65% and funding from other revenue sources are used to help low- income families with housing, veterans' welfare, cultural heritage and art promotion, and other public works.
